Debug Support and Known Limitations

Software bugs

Although MOPAC is routinely maintained in the status of no known software bugs, problems do occur. If a user suspects that there is a bug in the program, assistance can be requested by telephone or E-mail, with E-mail being preferred. Suspected bugs are given a high priority, and most are resolved within one working day. In the case of genuine bugs, a bug-fix or patch is provided to the user.

Known Limitations

The following list identifies faults or limitations in the current MOPAC.  These items have not been corrected up to now for the following reasons:
(A) The problem would require more work than can be justified at this time.  In other words, the problem is difficult to correct.
(B) the problem is not regarded as having a high enough priority.
If you are of the opinion that a bug on this list should be corrected, please inform me (openmopac@gmail.com).
If a limitation of MOPAC is identified, and communicated to me, I will either add it to this list, or have the limitation corrected (removed).

1. Excited state Mulliken populations are incorrect.  They use the wavefunction from the SCF, not the C.I. The Coulson density is correct.  This problem was discovered and reported by: Victor I. Danilov, Department of Quantum Biophysics, Academy of Sciences of the Ukraine, Kiev 143, Ukraine.
