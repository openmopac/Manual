Total COSMO Charge

The TOTAL COSMO CHARGE printed when COSWRT is used in a COSMO calculation is the total COSMO polarization charge on the surface of the solute, i.e., it is approximately equal to the negative of the total solute charge.  For detailed information, see p.140 of "COSMO-RS: From Quantum Chemistry to Fluid Phase Thermodynamics and Drug Design", by Andreas Klamt, COSMOlogic GmbH&CoKG, Leverkusen, Germany.  Publisher: Elsevier, 2005
